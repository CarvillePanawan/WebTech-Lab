About Us

We are a group of students from Saint Louis University, composed of individuals taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology(BSIT) and Bachelor of Science in Computer Science(BSCS). This group of ours is advocating for environmental protection, gender equality and human rights for the barangay Upper Magsaysay.
