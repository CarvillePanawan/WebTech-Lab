Introduction

The barangay Magsaysay Private Rode is located near the Magsaysay Avenue and it's barangay hall can be seen at 358 Magsaysay Avenue just under the fly over. The barangay is composed of 1065 residents, according to the 2010 NSO Census of Population.

Mission

To give quality public service to alleviate poverty, eliminate pollution and crime through good governance, in order to uplift the well-being of the constituents.

Vision

A most secure, progressive and healthy community of cooperation citizens.
